How Promote & Earn Works

A step-by-step guide to help you get started with promoting and earning coins

How It Works

For Promoters

Promoters help spread the word about our platform and earn coins in return. Here's how you can get started:

  1. Create an Account: Sign up with your email address and create a personalized account. It's quick and easy!
  2. Purchase Coins: Buy coins to begin promoting. Coins are used for boosting your promotions on the platform.
  3. Promote Your Social Media: Start promoting your social media accounts, such as Instagram, Twitter, or YouTube, to attract followers and increase your earnings.
  4. Track Your Progress: Use your dashboard to track how your promotions are performing and adjust strategies for better results.
  5. Earn Rewards: As your social media grows and you achieve your promotion goals, you'll earn more coins, unlocking new opportunities for growth.

Pro Tip: The more active you are in promoting, the higher your chances of earning coins faster!

For Earners

Earners complete tasks and earn coins. It's simple, fun, and rewarding!

  1. Choose Tasks: Browse through a variety of tasks available on the platform. Tasks could include anything from watching videos, liking posts, to participating in surveys.
  2. Complete Tasks: Select tasks that interest you and complete them according to the guidelines. Make sure to follow instructions carefully for successful completion.
  3. Earn Coins: Upon completion, you'll be rewarded with coins. The more tasks you complete, the more you can earn!
  4. Withdraw Earnings: Once you've accumulated enough coins, you can withdraw your earnings to your preferred payment method.
  5. Level Up: Completing more tasks helps you unlock higher-paying tasks and additional opportunities for income.

Pro Tip: Stay active and try completing as many tasks as possible to maximize your earnings!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started as a Promoter?

To get started, simply create an account, purchase coins, and begin promoting your social media accounts. The more you promote, the more you earn!

How can I withdraw my earnings?

Once you've earned enough coins, you can withdraw them via PayPal, bank transfer, or other supported methods directly from your dashboard.

Are there any fees for withdrawing coins?

No, there are no fees for withdrawing your earnings. However, some payment providers may charge transaction fees.
